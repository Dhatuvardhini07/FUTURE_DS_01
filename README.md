## Business Sales Performance Analytics (Task 1)

## Project Overview:
This project focuses on analyzing business sales data to understand performance trends and support data-driven decision making. The objective was to explore sales patterns, identify top-performing products and regions, and present insights using an interactive Power BI dashboard.
This project was completed as part of practical learning in business analytics and dashboard development.

## Objectives:
Analyze business sales data
Identify important sales patterns and trends
Create an interactive dashboard
Generate insights and business recommendations

## Key Analysis Performed:

Revenue Trends-
Analysis of how sales change over time, including year-wise and month-wise growth to understand overall revenue performance.

Top Selling Products-
Identification of products contributing the highest revenue and comparison of product performance.

High Value Customers-
Analysis to determine customers generating the most business value.

Regional Performance-
Comparison of sales performance across different countries or regions to identify strong and weak markets.

## Dashboard Components:
Total Sales (KPI Card)
Sales Trend (Line Chart)
Product Performance (Bar Chart)
Country/Region Performance (Pie Chart)
Top Customers (Table Visualization)

## Tools and Technologies Used:
Microsoft Power BI
Microsoft Excel
Python (basic data handling)

## Project Workflow:
Imported dataset into Power BI
Performed data cleaning and preparation
Created required DAX measures
Designed dashboard visuals
Generated business insights
Provided recommendations
Final dashboard formatting and reporting

## Outcome:
This project demonstrates how sales data can be transformed into meaningful insights through visualization and analysis. The dashboard enables quick understanding of business performance and supports informed decision making.
